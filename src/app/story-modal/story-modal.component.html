The peace of the land of Hi-Rule has been disturbed by an evil presence from the West.
Far off in the land of Gerado Plains, the evil Goonan has hatched a plan to steal
the powerful artifact of the gods, the QuadForce. He plans to use the power of the
QuadForce to control all of Hi-Rule and bend it to his will.

Lenk, a brave youngster of the Kokori Forest, must come forth and fulfill his destiny.

To save Hi-Rule and its people, he must defeat Goonan,
retrieve the QuadForce, and save the Princess Zilda.
